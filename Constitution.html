Preamble
We the entire students of Industrial and Production Engineering department of Federal University of Technology, Akure (FUTA) having unanimously resolved together to come together as a body with the purpose of enlightening and working as a unit to promote technological advancement hereby establish and give ourselves this constitution which shall be called the Constitution of the Association of Industrial and Production Engineering Students (AIPES).

Article 1

Supremacy, Name, Motto & Affiliation

Section 1 — Supremacy

(1) The constitution is supreme and its provisions shall have binding force on all members of the Association.

(2) Any law, directive or action inconsistent with the provisions of this constitution shall be null and void to the extent of its inconsistency.

Section 2 — Name

The Association shall be known and addressed as: "Association of Industrial and Production Engineering Students (AIPES) FUTA, Branch."

Section 3 — Motto

"Advancing the Cause of Technology"

Section 4 — Affiliation

The association is an affiliation of the Nigeria Society of Engineers (N.S.E).

Article 2

Aims & Objectives

Section 1
  • To promote unity among the members.
  • To create a forum whereby knowledge and ideas can be exchanged between members and problems affecting the department discussed.
  • To improve the standard of technology.
  • To bring to the awareness of the society the importance of industrial and production engineering.
  • To ensure maximum utilization of our limited resources.
  • To find a means by which we can put across views and knowledge to the society.
Article 3

Conformity with N.S.E By-Law

Section 1

The relation of this association to the Nigeria Society of Engineers (N.S.E) shall be governed at all times by the constitution, by-laws and rules of the N.S.E. Any by-laws not in accordance is unconstitutional.

Article 4

Membership

Section 1 — Eligibility

Membership shall be open to all undergraduate students of the Department of Industrial and Production Engineering, FUTA.

Section 2 — Duties of Members
  • All members are obliged to participate effectively and regularly in the activities of the association.
  • At the beginning of every session, every member — new and returning — shall pay the association dues and other levies agreed upon by 2/3 of the members before being allowed to register.
  • All student union representatives of the association shall give a report of meeting outcomes at every general meeting.
Section 3 — Code of Conduct

Every member is expected to conduct themselves in an orderly manner at all general meetings of the association and the university community in general. Any erring member shall face the disciplinary committee appointed by the executive committee.

Article 5

Patron / Matron

Section 1 — Appointment

Any individual who associates with the advancement of Industrial and Production Engineering in our society shall be deemed fit to be appointed as a Patron or Matron of the association.

Section 2 — Duties
  • Shall be an adviser to the association.
  • Shall attend the general meeting of the association when necessary.
  • Shall assist the association both morally and financially.
Section 3 — Staff Advisers

There shall be two staff advisers for the association from the Department of Industrial and Production Engineering, Federal University of Technology, Akure.

Article 6

Executive Committee

Section 1 — The 11 Offices
The President Vice President Secretary General Asst. Secretary General Financial Secretary PRO I PRO II Treasurer Welfare Director D.O Sport D.O Social
Section 2 — General Duties
  • Shall set up various committees as deemed necessary and specify their duties.
  • Shall meet from time to time to discuss vital issues.
Section 3 — Individual Officer Duties
The President
  • Shall preside over all general meetings of the association.
  • Shall summon meetings of the general assembly through the secretary general.
  • Shall be a co-signatory to all financial dealings.
  • Shall co-ordinate the activities of the association.
  • Shall be the chief spokesman of the association.
Vice President
  • Shall assist the president in all duties.
  • Shall carry out other duties assigned by the executive council.
  • Shall deputize for the president in their absence.
Secretary General
  • Shall be responsible for all secretarial duties.
  • Shall summon general meetings at the request of the president or at least 1/3 of members.
  • Shall keep the attendance book of all general meetings.
  • Shall present a semester report on activities at the last general meeting.
Assistant Secretary General
  • Shall assist the secretary general in all duties.
  • Shall perform the duties of the secretary general in their absence.
  • Shall document minutes of executive meetings.
  • Shall maintain the register of all members.
Financial Secretary
  • Shall be responsible for keeping financial records of the association.
  • Shall be a co-signatory to the association's account.
  • Shall perform other duties assigned by the executive committee.
Public Relation Officer I (PRO I)
  • Shall publicize all activities of the association with executive approval.
  • Shall maintain communication links with similar associations in and outside the university.
  • Shall be the chairman of the Press Committee.
  • Shall prepare association bulletins on proposed activities and achievements.
  • Shall speak to the public on behalf of the association if delegated by the president.
  • Shall keep contact with the alumni of the association regularly.
Public Relation Officer II (PRO II)
  • Shall assist PRO I in all duties.
  • Shall assist in emergency response planning and updating.
  • Shall manage calendar activities and media lists.
  • Shall maintain the PRO I database and tracking metrics.
  • Shall participate in brainstorming sessions.
Treasurer
  • Shall keep in safe custody all revenue of the association.
  • Shall prepare semester budgets for association activities.
  • Shall be a co-signatory to the association's account.
  • Shall perform other duties assigned by the executive committee.
Welfare Director
  • Shall ensure efficient health services for all members.
  • Shall be responsible for adequate supply of water, electricity, quality food and related welfare matters.
  • Shall be the chairman of the welfare committee.
Director of Sport (D.O Sport)
  • Shall be the chairman of the union's sport committee.
  • Shall co-ordinate games and sporting activities.
  • Shall seek the association's participation in university sport competitions.
  • Shall publish updates on sporting activities within the university community and beyond.
Director of Social (D.O Social)
  • Shall be responsible for organizing all social activities.
  • Shall be the chairman of the social committee.
  • Shall ensure indoor recreational facilities are provided for members.
  • Shall co-ordinate and review activities of students' societies, clubs and organizations.
Article 7

Electoral Committee

Section 1 — Electoral Rules
  • Elections shall be conducted at least two weeks after the NUESA election of every academic session.
  • All members are eligible to vote.
  • Voting shall be by secret ballot.
  • Only matriculated members can contest elections.
  • There shall be bye elections for any vacant office(s) in the executive committee.
Section 2 — Qualifications for Election
  • The post of President shall come from the 300 level.
  • Must be a bona fide financial member of AIPES FUTA Chapter.
  • Must be a minimum age of 16 years; for presidency, must be at least 18 years of age.
  • Must possess a CGPA of not less than 3.5.
  • Part four and five students are not eligible to contest for any post.
  • Any member who fails to attend 2/3 of general meetings is not eligible to contest.
  • Must have satisfied the requirements of the electoral committee.
Section 3 — Resignation & Removal from Office
  • Any officer wishing to resign shall give at least 30 days' official notice, accepted by 2/3 of the general assembly.
  • Any officer found guilty of misconduct or abuse of office shall be removed by the disciplinary committee.
  • The president shall approve every resignation letter with a signatory remark.
  • Any elected officer seeking re-election into any elective post shall first resign their present appointment.
  • No executive member is permitted to hold any other official position at school or university level while in office.
  • Any elected officer absent from the swearing-in ceremony and first business meeting without good cause shall be deemed to have forfeited their office.
  • The president may be impeached if found guilty of misconduct or misuse of power after proper investigation.
Section 4 — Stakeholders
  • Shall consist of the president, past presidents, honourables, class reps across levels and other notable leaders in the department.
  • Shall serve as the overseer of the central executive.
  • Shall attend to executive misunderstandings and problems.
  • Shall attend to other departmental duties if need be.
Section 5 — Bye Election

Vacancies during a school year shall be filled at a called election at a regular meeting within 15 days of the vacancy.

Article 8

Activities

Section 1 — Annual Activities

The activities of the association shall include a welcome party/orientation for new intakes during the first semester and the association's week, held annually. It shall feature:

  • Symposium
  • Orientation
  • Sport Games
  • Dinner
  • Projects
Article 9

Banking

Section 1

The association shall operate with any of the local commercial banks. The association shall operate a "Savings Account".

Article 10

Amendment

Section 1 — Amendment Process
  • Any registered member of the association can propose the amendment of any section(s) of this constitution, supported by 2/3 of members.
  • The president shall proceed to constitute a review committee for the amendment/review.
Adoption Notice
This constitution was amended, reviewed and unanimously adopted at the general meeting of the association held on 16th June 2017 in the university premises by members of the association, and shall come into effect immediately.
